<Info>
	Follow these steps to create a new form in VitaOne. Forms are our premier document generation tool.
</Info>

## Steps to Create a Form

<Steps>
	<Step title="Navigate to Forms">
		From the top left, open the <b>Your Apps</b> dropdown and select <b>Forms</b>.<br />
		This will take you to the Forms page, where you can see a list of all created forms.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Start a New Form">
		In the top right corner, click the green <b>Create Form</b> button.<br />
		Fill out the required information:
		<ul>
			<li><b>Name</b>: (Required) The name of your form</li>
			<li><b>Category</b>: (Optional) Select a category for your form, or leave blank</li>
			<li><b>Expires</b>: (Required) Set an expiration date for the form</li>
		</ul>
		After saving, youâ€™ll be taken to the form creation and editing page for your new form.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Customize and Publish">
		Use the form designer to add fields, customize the layout, and configure settings as needed. For more information click on the options below.<br />
		Once you're satisfied with your form, click the <b>Save & Publish</b> button to make it live and accessible to users.

		</Card>
		</Columns>
	</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
	You can edit your form later by clicking on your form from the main page list view ( Home / Forms).
</Tip>
